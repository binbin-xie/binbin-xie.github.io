About Me

I am a tenure-track assistant professor University of Texas at Arlington as a tenure-track assistant professor in August 2024. I'm looking for highly-motivated PhD students.

I am a PhD candidate at College of Information and Computer Sciences, University of Massachusetts Amherst under the supervision of Prof. Jie Xiong. I also work closely with Prof. Deepak Ganesan. My research is supported by Google PhD Fellowship.

Research Interests: Wireless Sensing, Mobile Health, Cyber-physical Systems, Smart IoT, and Wireless Networking
